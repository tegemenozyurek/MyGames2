Boost

The goal of the game Boost is to guide your rocket from the starting point to the landing spot without hitting any obstacles. It's not easy! Use the A and D keys to change the rocket's direction, and press SPACE to give it a boost. There are 10 levels, and you only have 3 lives to complete them. If you lose all 3 lives, you have to start over. This game holds significant value for R.exe among our projects. It stands out as our only 3D game, the first one to incorporate sound effects, our initial fully playable game, and the first one we released online, making it one of our most valuable creations.

Pingu's Fish

"Pingu's Fish" is the most popular game made by R.exe Games. It's a simple and enjoyable game that anyone, even those who haven't played games before, can easily play and love. The game has calming music in the background, gentle sound effects, uncomplicated graphics that don't strain the eyes, and easy gameplay. The goal of the game is to survive by moving Pingu left with the A key and right with the D key, avoiding snowballs falling from above. You also need to collect fish to increase your score and grab falling lives to stay alive longer. The main aim is to achieve the highest score.

Samurai Blade

"Samurai Blade" is a game we made quickly, all done at Heidelberg University in Germany. In the game, your samurai has to dodge endless sawblades coming randomly from two spawners above and bouncing off the walls. The only way to destroy these deadly sawblades is to jump over them and safely land back on the ground. Each destroyed sawblade adds 1 to your score, and the goal is to get the highest score. The key move in the game is the double jump. Use it wisely, guess well where the sawblades are going, and jump over them to stay alive.

2 Prince 1 Crown

"2 Prince 1 Crown" is a game created in 48 hours for the Yaşar University Mobile App Development Student Club GameJam (MUG GameJam). In this project, 2 artists and 1 developer from R.exe worked together to complete a game with a "competition" theme. The game is a split-screen beat 'em up where two players try to stay alive by defeating three different creatures that randomly appear on the screen. Players can collect power-ups to improve their abilities and use power-downs to slow down and freeze their opponent. They can also drop rocks and objects on their opponent by manipulating the environment.

Ice Hockey 2D

"Ice Hockey 2D" was created by R.exe Games in just 3 hours. The game is designed for 2 players. At the beginning of the game, the puck moves in a random direction. Both players try to defend their goals by moving them up and down. Although the puck starts relatively slow, it accelerates with each player or wall contact, making it challenging for players to defend their goals. The player who reaches a score of 3 wins the match.

Pixel Dungeons

Pixel Dungeons is a rogue-lite game still in development. It's planned to be released around the middle of 2024. In the game, after clearing monsters in a dungeon, you choose one of three doors to develop your character and try to go as far as you can. The goal is to give players a different experience each time based on their choices. The game aims to be released on a public platform by the end of 2024.
